Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know.

Common questions about Leadership Summit: who should attend, what to expect, and how to plan for the event.

Who should attend?

Leadership Summit is designed primarily for Signal franchise owners, executive directors, directors, and other key employees within franchise organizations.

Can I bring members of my leadership team?

Yes. Leadership Summit was intentionally expanded to allow franchise leadership teams to attend together.

What types of sessions will be offered?

Sessions will include executive team updates, leadership development, sales and operational breakout trainings, franchise owner-led best practices, and interactive discussions built around the day-to-day realities of running a Signal franchise.

What is included with registration?

Registration includes access to Summit sessions, breakout trainings, networking opportunities, and scheduled activities. Hotel accommodations are not included. Additional registration details will be shared during the registration process.

Do I need to attend all three days?

We strongly recommend participating in the full Summit to take part in the complete schedule of events.

Is the October 5 kickoff required?

No, but arriving by X:XX on October 5 allows you to participate in the Kids Against Hunger event and evening at Vala’s Pumpkin Patch.

Where will Leadership Summit be held?

Leadership Summit will take place at Signal Home Office in Omaha, Nebraska.
